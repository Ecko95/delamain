<threat_model>
## Security Threat Model

| Threat | Severity | Mitigation |
|--------|----------|------------|
| T-03-01: Accidental peer termination from a single ambiguous keypress. | high | Use `x` to enter kill-confirm mode, show selected peer id/name, require `enter` to confirm and `escape` to cancel. |
| T-03-02: Keyboard handlers consume Ctrl+C without cleanup. | medium | Centralize quit handling and always call renderer cleanup plus interval cleanup. |
| T-03-03: Manual smoke hides regressions in non-dashboard CLI/MCP behavior. | medium | Full `npm test` remains mandatory before summary. |
</threat_model>
